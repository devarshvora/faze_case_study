# FaZe Media Case Study

Data-driven analysis and strategic recommendations for FaZe Media's evolution into a full-service talent agency.

## The Challenge

FaZe Media needed comprehensive guidance across three critical areas:
1. Build a technology toolstack to manage a growing creator roster
2. Identify emerging creators for strategic representation
3. Analyze branded partnerships and campaign performance

---

## What I Did

### Task 1: Talent Agency Toolset Recommendation

Conducted functional analysis of FaZe Media's operational needs and built a comprehensive toolstack covering 5 strategic areas with 28+ recommendations:

**Data Analysis Work:**
- Mapped operational requirements across talent management, content creation, distribution, analytics, and collaboration
- Prioritized tools by scalability, integration capability, and ROI
- Built 4-phase implementation roadmap ($2K-6K monthly investment) showing break-even in first week of creator revenue
- Created cost-benefit analysis for tool selection across different agency growth stages

**Tools Recommended:** Karat Talent, Adobe Creative Cloud, Tableau, Hootsuite, Slack, Asana, Social Blade, Brand Safety.ai, and 20+ others

---

### Task 2: Creator Scouting & Strategic Signing

Built a **data-driven scoring model** to evaluate and rank emerging creators, resulting in 5 recommendations with strategic signing paths:

**Data Analysis Work:**
- Engineered composite scoring model using 25+ metrics across 4 platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, Instagram)
- Collected multi-dimensional creator data: follower counts, engagement rates, growth trajectories, audience demographics, brand safety
- Developed weighted scoring algorithm: FaZe Fit (35%) + Audience Overlap (25%) + Monetization Potential (25%) + Growth Trajectory (10%) + Brand Safety (5%)
- Set quantified thresholds for Direct Roster vs. Umbrella Agency signing paths
- Ranked 50+ creators and recommended top 5 with detailed justification
